About Teddy Bears

The teddy bear has been a beloved symbol of childhood and comfort for well over a century. Its origins trace back to 1902 when President Theodore"Teddy" Roosevelt famously spared a bear during a hunting trip in Mississippi. The story made headlines, inspiring a political cartoon and catching th eattention of a Brookly shopkeeper who crafted a plush bear and displayed it as "Teddy's bear." The name stuck, and the teddy bear was born, a gentle toy that would soon find its way into nurseries, gift boxes, and hearts around the globe.

From that first handmade bear to today's vast world of plush companions, teddy bears have remained a timeless gift. They cross generations and cultures, passed from grandparents to grandchildren, collected by hobbyists, cherished by children who whisper secrets to them at bedtime. some bears become keepsakes that travel the world with their owners, appearing in family photos for decades.

At Teddy Bears, we believe every bear should bring warmth, joy, and a sense of safety. Our team carefully designs and curates a wide variety of bears, from traditional stitched brown bears to modern, themed, and seasonal bears for holidays and special occasions. Each bear is crafted with premium materials, soft fabrics, and attention to detail that makes it feel truly one of a kind.

We work with dedicated artisans and trusted manufacturers to ensure every seam is sturdy, every paw is plush, and every nose is perfectly stitched. Whether you choose a classic bear for a baby shower, a birthday bear wearing a party hat, or a limited edition collectible for your display shelf, you can feel confident knowing you're bringing home a bear made with love and care.

But our story doesn't end with the bears themselves. We believe in building memories. Many of our customers share stories of their first teddy bear, the one they held close through childhood adventures and nighttime fears. Some even pass that same bear down, worn but well-loved, to the next generation, a reminder that something simple and soft can hold so much love across time.

Today, our community to teddy bear enthusiasts spans the globe. We host teddy bear picnics, birthday club surprises, and limited edition drops that keep collectors excited all year long. We love hearing your stories and seeing your photos, from babies hugging their first bear to adults adding a new bear to a lifelong collection.

We're more than just a shop. W're caretakers of a tradition that started more than 120 years ago, a tradition built on kindness, warmth, and the belief that everyone deserves something soft to hold onto. when you choose one of our teddy bears, you're choosing more than a toy. You're choosing a friend, a keepsake, and a tiny piece of history stitched with care.

Thank you for being part of our story. whether you're here to find the perfect first bear for a newborn, a comforting gift for a friend in need of a smile, or a collectible to treasure for years, we're honored you've chosed to share that moment with us. Together, we'll keep the spirit of the teddy bear alive for generations to come, one hug at a time
