Overview

Purpose

This site is designed for families, parents and children who love teddy bears. Its purpose is to share warm, family-friendly information about teddy bears, display a small gallery, and help kids and parents explore teddy bear fun in a safe, soft, welcoming web space.

Audience

The target audience is families with young children, teddy bear enthusiasts, and parents looking for kid-friendly content online.

Dynamic Elements

This site will include a responsive navigation bar and an interactive image gallery for teddy bears, managed with basic JavaScript.

Branding

Website Logo

The Teddy bears Website logo will feature a simple, friendly teddy bear icon next to the site "Teddy Bears" in a soft, rounded font. The colors will match the site palette - soft pastels with accents of pinks and blues, and some will have different variations of the colors and accents depending on the page

Content

Home Page

The Home page will welcome families and children with a friendly headline, a hero teddy bear image, a short introduction paragraph, and a gallery grid of teddy bear photos. It will link to other pages through a clear navigation menu.

About/Child Page

The About or Products page will provide more details about teddy bears — their history, types, or products offered. It will include a headline, supporting text, and at least one relevant teddy bear image. The page will share the same header and footer for a consistent look and link back to the Home page.

Home Page Wireframes

The Home page includes a header with logo and nav, a large hero banner image, a short welcome message, and a grid gallery of teddy bear images. The footer keeps site credits and mataches the style.
