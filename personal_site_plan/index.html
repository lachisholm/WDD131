4. Color Palette

The colors are soft pastels suitable for a child's eye, with a solid navy blue accent for emphasis.

  • Primary Background: FDF6F0 (Very light cream pastel)
  • Secondary Accent: F8D5C2 (Soft peach pastel for header/nav)
  • Text Color:#333333 (Deep neutral gray for readability)
  • Accent Color: 004C7F (Solid navy blue for hover states, buttons, or links)

6. Wireframes

Home Page Wireframes

The home page includes a header with the logo and navigation links, a hero banner image of a teddy bear, a short welcome headline and paragraph, and a grid gallery of teddy bear images. A footer at the bottom has site credits.

Child Page Wireframe

The child page follows the same header and footer style, with a headline for "Products" or "About Teddy Bears", a short paragraph describing teddy bears in detail, and one supporting supporting image. Navigation stays consistent and mobile responsive.

7. Content Guidelines

The site will use clear, short paragraphs with warm, friendly language. Images will include 'alt' text for accessibility. Navigation links will connect the home page to the child page and back. The hamberger menu will make the nav mobile-friendly.
